About Us

The Story Behind Volton: Smart Energy Management for Every Home

Volton was born out of a desire to make energy management smarter, more accessible, and more cost-effective for homeowners. Our journey began when our founders, a group of passionate engineers and entrepreneurs, realized the untapped potential of combining smart technology with energy consumption data.

Driven by the belief that everyone should have the tools to optimize their energy usage and save money, we set out to develop a comprehensive solution. After months of rigorous research, development, and testing, Volton's smart plug ecosystem and intelligent software came to life.

Today, Volton is at the forefront of the smart home revolution, empowering homeowners across the globe to take control of their energy consumption. We continue to innovate and expand our offerings, always keeping our mission at the heart of everything we do: helping you consume energy smartly, not fearfully.
